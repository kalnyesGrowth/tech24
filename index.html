Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to help you understand our service process and capabilities.

What types of equipment does Tech24 service?
We service commercial cooking equipment (ovens, fryers, grills, steamers), refrigeration (walk-ins, reach-ins, ice machines), beverage systems, HVAC, and commercial electrical and plumbing systems. We work with nearly all major manufacturers.
How quickly can a technician arrive?
Tech24 offers same-day service in most metro markets. Our GPS-equipped technicians are dispatched from the nearest service location with fully stocked parts vans, minimizing downtime for your operation.
Do you offer preventive maintenance programs?
Yes. Our planned maintenance programs help you prevent costly breakdowns before they happen. Scheduled inspections, cleaning, and calibration keep your equipment running efficiently and extend its lifespan.
What is covered under your 90-day guarantee?
Our guarantee covers both parts and labor for 90 days from the date of repair. If the same issue recurs within that window, we return at no additional charge to resolve it.
Do you service my area?
Tech24 operates in 50+ metro markets with 100+ owned locations and a network of 1,600+ service partners covering the Western, Southwest, Midwest, Mid-Atlantic, Northeast, and Southeast regions. Contact us to confirm availability in your area.
How do I become a Tech24 service partner?
If you own a commercial foodservice equipment service company and are interested in joining the Tech24 family, contact us through the form below or call 888-774-4950. We are always looking for quality service partners to expand our network.
